What is it?

AN INTERACTIVE WORKSHOP DESIGNED TO EQUIP FIRST-YEAR OPEN MAJORS FOR SUCCESS.

Every year about 20% of new students enter the university as a declared “open major.” It can be overwhelming to sort through the wealth of information and opinions new students receive while trying to select a specific major. This workshop will encourage first-year students to explore their interests, values, and abilities as well as discover campus resources to help in identifying potential academic and career paths.
